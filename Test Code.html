How to make a Ham & Cheese Sandwich

  1. Acquire ingredients (Ham, Cheese, Bread of Choice, Condiments(optional))
  2. Remove two bread slices from loaf packaging
  3. Slather condiments on slices of bread to your heart's content if using them, otherwise proceed to next step
  4. Add as many slices of ham to the sandwich as you want to one of the slices of bread
  5. Add as many slices of cheese to the sandwich as you want to one of the slices of bread
  6. Press the slices of bread together with the meat and cheese between them
